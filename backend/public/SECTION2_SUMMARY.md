## 🚀 How It Works

Each quiz:
- ✅ Randomly selects **10 questions** from the 60-question bank
- ✅ Shuffles answer options for each question
- ✅ Provides **30 seconds** per question
- ✅ Shows instant feedback with explanations
- ✅ Tracks score in leaderboard
- ✅ Saves history for later review

This means you can take **many different quizzes** since only 10 of 60 questions are used each time!
